Day03: Phase-Triplet Relationship

Objective

Explore the relationship between surgical phases and triplet annotations in CholecT50 VID01.

Findings

Preparation

* grasper, grasp, gallbladder

Calot Triangle Dissection

* grasper, grasp, gallbladder
* hook, dissect, gallbladder
* bipolar, coagulate, blood_vessel

Clipping and Cutting

* clipper, clip, cystic_duct
* scissors, cut, cystic_duct

Gallbladder Dissection

* hook, dissect, gallbladder
* grasper, retract, liver

Gallbladder Packaging

* grasper, grasp, specimen_bag
* grasper, pack, gallbladder

Cleaning and Coagulation

* irrigator, aspirate, fluid

Gallbladder Extraction

* grasper, grasp, specimen_bag

Observation

Different phases are characterized by distinct triplet patterns.

This supports the idea that phase recognition can be inferred from surgical actions and interactions.

Note: The dataset uses the phase label “carlot-triangle-dissection”. This likely corresponds to Calot’s triangle dissection, and the spelling follows the original dataset annotation.
